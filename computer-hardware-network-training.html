Course Curriculum

The Computer Hardware and Networking course is meant to make you confident in practical work. You don’t just learn what a computer is. You learn how to open it, fix it, connect it, and get it working. The course includes computer hardware and networking, trade theory, practical training, and workshop practice.

You will learn about computer parts, operating systems, computer networks, and problem-solving techniques. This Computer Hardware and Networking course develops skills that are important in practical work.

Theory & Practical: Students learn the basic structure of a computer, including CPU, motherboard, RAM, hard disk, SMPS, and peripheral devices.

Sound Concepts: Clear concepts help learners diagnose problems without fear and work accurately.

Basic IT Concepts: Learn fundamental details like understanding system layouts and repair instructions.

Computer Hardware and Networking: Students learn about LAN, WAN, cables, routers, switches, and basic computer network setups.

Practical Training: Students practise installing operating systems, software, drivers, and antivirus tools.

Workshop Technology: Students work with real tools used in a hardware networking course.

Troubleshooting: SFind problems, understand symptoms, and fix hardware and networking issues.
