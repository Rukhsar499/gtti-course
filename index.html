Course Curriculum

The Automobile Engineering Diploma course outline is structured in such a way as to give the students confidence and skills slowly but surely.

The Indian Automotive Industry is one of the fastest-growing markets of the world and contributes significantly to the country's manufacturing eco-system. The future workforce of this industry will need to work on a series of non-routine tasks that require social intelligence, complex critical thinking and creative problem-solving ability.

Workshop Science and Calculation: The students learn about measurements and simple calculations that are used daily while working on vehicles.

Engineering Drawing: This part teaches them how to read and interpret the repair instructions provided through drawings.

Auto Theory: This module teaches them the functions of the vehicle inside and out.

Auto Transmission Theory: Students understand how the power from the engine travels to the wheels. It talks about gears, clutches, and different transmission systems in a very lucid manner.

Workshop Technology: Students work with tools, machines, and actual vehicle parts. They learn safety, discipline, and the correct way to handle equipment. This subject teaches patience and responsibility.

Automotive Diesel Theory: Students learn how a diesel engine functions, how it burns fuel, and produces power. Students will study the operation of injectors and pumps that are used in modern diesel systems.

The Workshop Practice: Students learn how a diesel engine functions, how it burns fuel, and produces power. Students will study the operation of injectors and pumps that are used in modern diesel systems.

Industrial Engineering and Motor Vehicle Act: This course provides them with knowledge about the discipline in a workshop environment and road regulations, respectively.

Auto Electricity: This part starts training them in wiring, batteries, and electronics.

The course includes the current systems such as MPFI, turbocharging, power brakes, anti-lock brakes, power steering, CRDI engines, car air conditioning, emission norms, handling customers, sales fundamentals, car insurance, and future alternate fuels.

The syllabus enables the students to perform the tasks in the automobile sector with efficiency, responsibility, and most importantly, with pride.

What skills will you gain from Automobile engineering

The student will acquire the confidence to understand how modern cars really work from the inside out. They will develop an understanding of how fuel systems, braking systems, power steering systems, and modern engine designs all combine to provide a safe and smooth ride on the road.

As future automobile engineers, they will also acquire skills in managing electrical and electronic controls, air conditioning systems, and Euro emission regulations. Additionally, the student will acquire valuable customer handling and sales skills related to car insurance, which will enable them to directly interact and deal with people.
