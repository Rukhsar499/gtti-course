Course Curriculum

The electronics and telecommunication engineering course based on practical applications. You are not merely learning theory. You are dealing with circuits, signals, devices, and systems. The course combines theory, practicals, workshop practice, and fault diagnosis.

Students are trained in electronics basics, communication systems, digital circuits, and networking fundamentals. This training instils patience, logic, and confidence in students. All these qualities are necessary in all electronics and telecommunication engineering jobs in Kolkata or elsewhere.

Trade Theory & Trade Practical: Understand the fundamentals of electronics, including current, voltage, resistance, diodes, transistors, and ICs.

Sound Electronics Concepts: Students comprehend the flow of signals and the functioning of devices.

Engineering Drawing:Understand how to read circuit diagrams, symbols, layouts, and block diagrams in electronics and communication systems

Electronics and Telecommunication Systems: SUnderstand the basics of communication and networking concepts applied in practical systems.

Practical Training: Involves testing electronic boards, signal verification, fault detection, and system implementation under expert supervision.

Workshop Technology: Understand the application of electronic tools, soldering machines, measuring devices, and safety devices.

Troubleshooting: Detect and rectify errors in electronic circuits and communication systems.
