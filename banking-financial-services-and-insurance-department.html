Course Curriculum

The Finance & Insurance Course is all about practical learning. You don’t just learn by heart. You learn how money flows. You learn how businesses keep accounts, calculate taxes, and process financial information.

Includes topics such as financial accounting, taxation, basic insurance, and computerised accounting. Students learn software such as Tally Prime and Advanced Excel, which are used in offices today.

The curriculum is designed to prepare students for professional courses in finance and accounting.

Basics of Financial Accounting: Students acquire the basics of a certificate in financial accounting.

Accounting Principles and Concepts: Students learn the reasons for accounting principles and concepts to maintain accurate financial statements.

Manual Accounting Practice: Students learn ledger posting, journal preparation, trial balance, and final accounts to gain confidence.

Computerised Accounting with Tally Prime: Certificate course in Tally Prime with GST, including billing, inventory, GST returns, and compliance.

Taxation and GST Basics: Includes income tax, GST concepts, and filing basics. It is useful for those searching for accounts and taxation courses in Kolkata.

Advanced Excel for Accounting: Acquire Excel tools from an advanced Excel training institute in Kolkata.

Insurance Fundamentals: Students acquire the basics of life insurance, general insurance, claims, and documentation.

Financial Security and Practical Tools: This module deals with financial security and practical tools.

Life and Non-life Insurance: Students acquire the basics of life insurance and general insurance.

Learn Documentation: They learn policies, claims, and customer documentation.

Advanced Excel: This module also deals with Advanced Excel, used for reporting, data management, and financial analysis.

Ready for Work: This module helps students to become job-ready for finance, insurance, and office jobs.
