Course Curriculum

The Air-Conditioning and Refrigeration Course gives you real, working skills. A few of these skills include Trade Theory & Practical Work and Commercial & Domestic Air-Conditioning Systems. You also learn Refrigeration Systems and Components and Workshop Technology. Learning about washing machine technology and workshop science is important in HVAC courses. You will learn Troubleshooting Skills, too. This training gives you the confidence to work with real HVAC systems.

Trade Theory & Trade Practical: Students learn the fundamentals of refrigeration systems, including the working principles of refrigeration cycles, system flow, and heat transfer.

Sound Concepts: THelp learners work accurately and confidently while servicing or installing systems where precision is critical.

Engineering Drawing:Students learn how to read, understand, and interpret technical drawings, symbols, and layouts related to refrigeration and air-conditioning systems.

Refrigeration and Air-conditioning: SFocusses on both commercial and domestic applications. Students understand how cooling systems operate in real-life settings.

Practical Training: Includes system operation, servicing techniques, and performance checks, helping students gain confidence in handling live systems under guided supervision.

Workshop Technology: Students work with tools, instruments, and equipment used in the AC & refrigeration industry. Builds technical discipline, safety awareness, and work practices.

Troubleshooting: Develops the most critical skill in the field, problem diagnosis. Students learn how to identify faults, analyse symptoms, and apply logical solutions efficiently.

What skills will you gain from refrigeration and air conditioning courses?

When you learn about refrigeration and air conditioning, you don’t merely learn a trade. You build confidence in your own hands. You learn to install and repair air conditioners, refrigerators, and cooling systems used in homes, shops, and hospitals.

You understand how compressors, gases, and electrical components interact. Learning technical skills will help you learn troubleshooting. The most important thing you will learn besides your technical skills will be discipline and responsibility. Learning how to effectively interact with customers from an AC repairing training centre will also help you gain their trust and respect, and develop a steady income.
