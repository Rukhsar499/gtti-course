Course Curriculum

This beautician course is all about learning by doing. You do not just watch beauty procedures. You learn them. You learn about skin. You learn to style hair. You learn to apply makeup. Students learn the science of beauty and salon practices. The course combines beauty knowledge with salon practices so that students feel confident handling clients.

This professional beauty parlour course is designed for students who want to work in salons or as self-employed beauticians.

Skin Anatomy & Care Foundations: Students are introduced to the anatomy and physiology of the skin.

Skin Treatments with Machines: Students are taught safety, hygiene, and proper procedures to achieve effective results.

Introduction to the Salon Industry:Students are introduced to salon work culture, handling clients, salon hygiene, and salon etiquette.

Hair Basics & Advanced Styling: Students are taught the structure of hair, hair care, basic and advanced haircuts, and various styling methods.

Makeup Fundamentals & Advanced Application: Skin preparation, product knowledge, and blending techniques are emphasised.

Hands-on Practice: This module helps students gain confidence through extensive practice under expert supervision.

Nail Art & Creative Expression: Students are taught basic nail care and creative nail art designs.

Professional Grooming Skills: Students are trained in posture, grooming, communication, and client comfort.

Work-Ready Training: This module trains students for employment in salons, freelance work, and independent beauty services.
