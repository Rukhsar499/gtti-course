Course Curriculum

The Electrical Technician diploma course focuses on the skills you can apply in real life. You do not just read about power and voltage. You see it, measure it, and work with it safely. The course includes theory, training, workshop practice, and troubleshooting.

Students learn about wiring systems, motors, transformers, electrical machines, and safety procedures in the Electrical Service Technician course . Discipline, alertness, and responsibility are very important qualities for an electrical technician.

Trade Theory & Trade Practical: The students learn the fundamentals of electricity, including voltage, resistance, and power.

Sound Electrical Concepts: Students understand the flow of electricity and the causes of faults.

Engineering Drawing: The students will learn to read electrical diagrams, symbols, and wiring layouts.

Electrical Installation & Maintenance: The students will learn residential, commercial, and simple industrial wiring.

Practical Training: Practical training will include wiring boards, panels, motors, and simple machines.

Workshop Technology: The students will learn to handle electrical tools, testers, multimeters, and safety devices.

Troubleshooting: The students learn to locate faults, diagnose, and logically repair electrical systems.
